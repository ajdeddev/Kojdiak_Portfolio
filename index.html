About Me

Creative Designer & Brand Strategist

With over 5 years of experience in graphic design and branding, I specialize in creating compelling visual identities that help businesses stand out in competitive markets. My approach combines strategic thinking with creative execution.

I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics but about solving problems and communicating values effectively. Every project starts with understanding the client's vision and ends with a solution that exceeds expectations.

Based in Slovakia, I work with clients worldwide, from startups to established companies, helping them build memorable brands that resonate with their target audience.

My Projects

Here are some of my most significant projects. Each of them represents a unique challenge and solution tailored to the client's specific needs.

Severe Weather Slovakia

SWS o. z.

I developed a functional application for Severe Weather Slovakia (SWS) o. z. The app, available at https://ajdeddev.github.io/mapsws/, serves as a browser-based tool designed to make creating weather forecasts for the territory of Slovakia easier and more efficient.

The application uses modern web technologies to display complex meteorological data in a clear and user-friendly interface.

View Project

Kooperativa Vienna Insurance Group

Advertising Posters

I created several posters for Kooperativa Vienna Insurance Group. My friend then showed them to the company's general director (Mária Š.), who liked them so much that she contacted me directly and decided to use the posters as official advertising.

The posters were printed as leaflets and displayed around the city. This project shows how personal initiative and creativity can lead to unexpected professional opportunities.

Tridentu

Startup Branding

I created the logo, visual identity, and mascot for the startup Tridentu. Since I was roommates with the founder, we agreed to collaborate and created the resulting branding concept.

Tridentu's identity reflects its innovative approach and dynamic nature. The logo and visual style were designed to appeal to the target audience and stand out from the competition.

Samuel Hula

Logo Creation

I designed this logo for Samuel Hula, where the symbol visually represents the initials S and H. The goal was to create a clean, modern mark that reflects his identity in a simple yet distinctive way. Since we have known each other since high school, where we were close friends as well as business partners, the collaboration felt natural and effortless.

We agreed on a deal in which I would create the logo for him, built on mutual trust and a shared understanding of his vision. The result is a bold, minimal logo with a personal story behind it, combining our long-standing connection with a professional design approach.

Dripura

Complete Branding

DRIPURA is my potentional clothing brand that I am working on, built around clean design, individuality, and confidence. I created the logo as a bold, minimal symbol that represents the core of the brand and works seamlessly across garments and visual identity.

Designed to be timeless rather than trend-driven, the mark reflects precision, movement, and modern expression. It represents the starting point of DRIPURA and my vision for a brand built to grow and define its own identity through clothing.
